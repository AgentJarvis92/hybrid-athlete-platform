# TrainStack
## The All-in-One Training Platform for Serious Athletes

---

# THE PROBLEM

Athletes today manage their training across 4-5 disconnected apps:

| App | Purpose | Cost |
|-----|---------|------|
| Runna | Running plans | $15/mo |
| Hevy | Strength training | $10/mo |
| Cronometer | Nutrition tracking | $10/mo |
| Cal AI | Photo food logging | $8/mo |
| TrueCoach | Trainer programming | $15/client/mo |
| **Total** | | **$58/mo** |

**None of these apps talk to each other.**

Your nutrition app doesn't know you ran 18 miles today. Your running app doesn't know your trainer scheduled heavy squats tomorrow. Your trainer can't see your nutrition or running data. You're making decisions with incomplete information — and so is your coach.

---

# THE SOLUTION

**TrainStack** is the first training platform that connects running, strength, and nutrition into one intelligent system — with a coach portal built in.

**One app. One dashboard. Everything connected.**

- 🏃 **Run** — Structured training plans, GPS tracking, audio coaching
- 🏋️ **Lift** — Workout logging, exercise library, progressive overload
- 🥗 **Eat** — Photo/voice/barcode food logging, AI-powered calorie estimation
- 🧠 **AI Coach** — Adjusts nutrition based on training load, reschedules missed sessions, predicts race times
- 💻 **Trainer Portal** — Your coach programs from a laptop, you execute from your phone

# HOW IT WORKS

### For the Athlete

Open the app. See your entire day:

**Today's Plan:**
- 6-mile tempo run (pace targets included)
- Upper body + core workout (programmed by your coach)
- Nutrition target: 2,650 cal (adjusted up because of today's run)
- "Tomorrow is your long run — carb-load tonight"

**Log your food in 5 seconds:**
- Snap a photo → AI estimates calories and macros
- Or say "chicken breast, rice, and broccoli" → logged
- Or scan a barcode → instant lookup

**Track your run:**
- GPS tracking with live pace and splits
- Audio cues: "You're 15 seconds ahead of target, ease back"
- Auto-syncs mileage to your weekly plan

**Log your workout:**
- Today's exercises pre-loaded by your trainer
- Tap to log sets, reps, weight
- Rest timer runs automatically
- PR notifications when you hit a new best

### For the Trainer/Coach

Open **trainstack.com/coach** on your laptop:

- **Build programs** with drag-and-drop weekly view
- **See everything** your client does — runs, lifts, meals, bodyweight
- **Adjust in real-time** — swap exercises, modify volume, leave notes
- **Push to client** — publish a program, it appears on their phone instantly
- **Track compliance** — "Kevin completed 85% of sessions, averaging 2,400 cal/day"

### The AI Layer (The Secret Sauce)

TrainStack's AI connects all three domains:

| Signal | AI Response |
|--------|------------|
| Ran 18 miles today | Increases calorie target by 800, suggests high-carb meals |
| Missed Tuesday's tempo run | Reschedules to Thursday, adjusts weekend long run |
| Heavy squats scheduled tomorrow | Moves interval run to a different day |
| Weight trending down + mileage up | "You're underfueling. Add 200 cal/day." |
| Race is 7 days away | Activates carb-loading protocol, reduces training |
| Protein intake consistently low | "You're averaging 120g. Target is 180g. Add a shake post-workout." |

**Weekly AI Summary:**
> "34 miles this week (target 35 ✅). 3/4 strength sessions. Nutrition 88% adherent. Predicted marathon: 4:05. Next week is peak mileage — I've bumped your daily calories to 2,800."
